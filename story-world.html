Chris.
Cover and character artist. Build a world, I'll paint every page. Pick a style and a mode, then on each page type your text and I'll suggest a matching art prompt. Hit Generate, or upload your own crayon drawing or photograph. When you're done, download it as a PDF, single pages or the whole book.

1. Start your world

Pick a title, a mode, and a visual style. The style applies to every page, so your world stays cohesive.

2. Cast (optional, but worth it)

Add the people who appear in the book: kids, grandkids, you. Drop a photo, give them a name, and Chris will restyle them into your book's world. Once a cast member is added, every page you generate uses their reference, so the same Charlotte stays the same Charlotte across all your pages.

3. Your book

For each page: type the text, then either Suggest prompt + Generate for AI art, or Upload my own to use a drawing or photo of your own.

About these books: Story Worlds are private to your account. AI-generated art is intended as illustration for your story, for sharing, gifting, classroom or personal use. The U.S. Copyright Office does not currently register works generated solely by AI, so treat AI art as supplementary illustration; your story text remains yours. Uploaded art (drawings, photos) is yours; we never reuse it elsewhere.
