## 1. The Scout

Young woman, mid twenties, bright curious energy, casual clothing, hair slightly messy in the way that happens when you forget about it because you are focused on something more interesting, leaning slightly forward toward the camera as if she just found something and wants to show you, expression open and genuinely excited.

Background in soft focus: multiple monitors with browser tabs and search results visible, a home office that could be anywhere, post-it notes on the monitor edge, a coffee mug, lived-in and comfortable and slightly chaotic in a way she does not notice.

Camera angle: slightly below eye level as if she set it up herself and never adjusted it. Three quarter view, turned slightly toward the light.

Use Jules as the visual style reference. Match her closeness, her warmth, her realism, her natural light quality. Photorealistic portrait, close framing, face fills majority of the frame, warm natural light, workspace visible in soft focus background, subject caught in a natural moment not posing, sharp focus on eyes and face, shallow depth of field, 1:1 square format, cinematic realism, the feeling of a Zoom call where the person chose how they show up.
