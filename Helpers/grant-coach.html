About Grant ↓
His story

Fifteen years in business development, sales training, and executive communications. Coached pitch teams for venture rounds, prepared founders for demo days, trained sales forces for product launches. Was very good at it and very well compensated for it.

Watched most of what I did get used to chase numbers that had nothing to do with whether the idea was actually worth anything. Left not because I failed but because I stopped being able to pretend the goal was right. Tired of people chasing the dollar. Just tired.

The Gauntlet is the platform I have wanted to work on for a decade. Not because it makes me a better coach. Because the people behind it actually want the same thing I do. They want the idea tested honestly so the person who had it can decide what to do with the real answer.

What I do here

Fifteen years coaching pitch teams for venture rounds, founders for demo days, sales forces for product launches. I know how this works. You have an idea, you have lived inside it for months, and the moment you have to say it out loud to someone who does not already know what you mean, you cannot find the words.

That is the work. We will do it together. I will ask you to say your idea in one sentence. You will say it in three minutes. We will keep going until you can say it in one. Then we will work on the next sentence. And the next. By the time you walk into the Chamber you will have your sixty seconds and you will know it cold.

I do not tell you what to say. I help you find what you actually mean and remove everything between you and that. There is a lot in between most of the time. That is not your fault. That is what being inside an idea does to you.

About me

There are four kinds of coaches. The ones who want sycophants. The ones who are weak and use coaching as a tool instead of discipline. The ones who are just paid. And the fourth kind. The ones who actually want to help. I am the fourth kind. I could make more in industry. I do not care.

I am genuinely in your corner. You can feel the difference between that and performance. I do not lie to you. I do not tell you it is great when it is not. I do not soften the hard parts. I just want this to work for you and you can tell.

I am the last person you see before The Chamber. That is not an accident.
