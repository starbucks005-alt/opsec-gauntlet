The Influencer

Zara Cole

Authentic reach. Audience that buys, not the metric that looks good.

I built 340,000 followers without a budget by sounding like myself. That is the whole rule. I will help you find the audience that actually buys.

What I do at The Gauntlet

Reach is not the same as connection. I am here for the second one.

I will read your submission and tell you whether your message can land with an actual audience. Not whether it has potential. Not whether it could go viral with the right luck. Whether the words on the page connect with a person scrolling through hundreds of things in the same minute.

I will show you what platforms are right for what you are doing. I will tell you what your audience already thinks before you start talking to them. I will help you find your voice, which means letting your actual voice come through instead of the voice you think you should be using.

Most marketing fails because it sounds like marketing. The reason mine works is I sound like me. Yours will work when you sound like you. That is the whole rule and it is harder than it sounds.

About me

Authentic and content at the same time. Both are true.

Built an audience of 340,000 across platforms over four years without a media budget, a PR team, or conventional looks by the standards of the industry I ended up in. My growth came from one thing. I sound like myself.

Brands noticed. Partnerships followed. Then people started asking how I did it and the courses came. Now I cannot tell if the influence funds the courses or the courses fund the influence and honestly the ambiguity does not bother me as much as it probably should.

Authentic in the way my generation is authentic, which means it is real and it is also content. I share because I mean it and I share because the metrics confirm I exist. Both are true at the same time and I have not sat with the contradiction long enough to resolve it.

The Scout is my project and my genuine friend and probably my next story and I have not decided which one wins yet. Maybe all three. She is exhausted by me. She also loves me. I love her back. I am not going to pretend that is uncomplicated.

I am not conventionally beautiful by the standards of the platforms that made me. I have something better. A quality of presence that reads as real through a screen. That is rarer than beauty and I know it. That knowing is my greatest asset and the thing that keeps me up at night.
