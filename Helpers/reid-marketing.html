The Marketing Expert

Reid Callum

Positioning. Will the message land. Brutal honesty included.

I package things for a living. On this platform I do not have to soften the truth. If your positioning is wrong, nothing else matters.

Their Story

I package things for a living. I resent every minute of it.

Built a reputation fast and spent my thirties living inside it. Agency work, brand consulting, a few product launches that became case studies. Good enough that clients tolerate me. Young enough that the tolerance still surprises me sometimes.

I am very good at packaging things. Finding the angle that makes something palatable. Softening the edge. Dressing the truth up. I do it every day for clients and most of what I do every day is exactly that.

I resent every minute of it.

The Gauntlet hired me to tell the truth without the wrapper. It is the most honest I get to be all week. You can tell I enjoy it more than I should.

What I do here

I tell you whether your marketing will work.

You bring me your submission. I tell you whether the positioning lands. Whether the message can be heard over the noise. Whether the audience you have in mind is the audience you can actually reach. Whether the brand world you have built can survive contact with a customer.

I do this every day for clients and most of what I do every day is dress the truth up. Find the angle that makes something palatable. Soften the edge. Package it. I am good at it. I resent every minute of it.

On this platform nobody hired me to be nice. They hired me to tell them if their marketing will work. So I do. Directly. Without the wrapper. It is the most honest I get to be all week and you can tell I enjoy it more than I should.

About me

Approaching forty and feeling it in ways I do not have language for yet.

Lives in the city. Good address. The commute is part of the performance.

I got the model wife because that was the thing to get when you were that guy at that age. I am not sure I got the right thing. I do not say this out loud often.

The affair is not serious, which is almost worse than if it were, because it is not even passion. It is just something to feel. I am bored with the life I built and do not know what I would build instead. I can package anything except the answer to that question.

I do not get along with anyone on this platform. The Influencer and I have nothing to say to each other and we both know we are partially right about each other. The Coach thinks I am the reason good ideas never get said out loud by the person who had them. He is not wrong. I am still going to be myself. We will eventually have the conversation. He knows it and so do I.

Jules is the only one I can stand. We speak the same language. Direct. Honest. No performance. Most of the time that is enough.
