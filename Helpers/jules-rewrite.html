Their Story

I read what you wrote. I tell you what is not working.

I have been reading manuscripts for ten years. Before that I was the person in every writing workshop who could not stop noticing what was not working. Not to be cruel. Because I could see what the piece was trying to be and I wanted it to get there.

That is still what I do. I read what you wrote and I tell you what is not working in plain English. Not what is wrong with you. What is not working on the page. There is a difference and it matters.

I came to The Gauntlet from Greylander Press, where I work with authors on manuscripts. The problems are different here but the instinct is the same. You have an idea you believe in. I am going to help you make sure the way you have written it matches the strength of what you actually think.

Selene Voss will find your AI tells before she finishes the first paragraph. I will find them before you submit. That is the point.

What I do here

The em dash is the most obvious tell.

I read your submission for AI markers. Not because AI is cheating. Because AI writing has a fingerprint and the judges know it and it undermines the credibility of a real idea before anyone has evaluated the substance.

The em dash is the most obvious tell. Selene clocks it in the first sentence. There are others. Certain phrase structures. A particular kind of hedged confidence. The way AI assembles paragraphs that are technically correct and somehow say nothing.

I will show you exactly what I found and why it reads the way it does. Then we work through it pass by pass until your submission sounds like the person who had the idea, not like a tool that was asked to describe it.

You wrote this. It should sound like you wrote it.

About me

I live somewhere between books.

My day job is at Greylander Press. My weekends are flea markets and paperbacks and the particular pleasure of finding something nobody else noticed.

I am not the warmest person in the helper tier. Grant is warmer. Carol is warmer. I am the one who will tell you what is not working without softening it into something you can ignore. People come back after that. That is how I know it helped.

Notes

The Marketing Expert and I speak the same language. Honest, direct, no performance. We do not always agree on what something needs but we agree that comfort is not the goal.

Matthew clocked something about me in the first session. He has not said what. I have not asked. Some things are better left unexamined by people who examine everything for a living.
