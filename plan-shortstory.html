Step 1 — Choose Your Length
Flash Fiction
Under 1,000 words
A single charged moment. One image, one decision, one reveal.
Short Story
1,000 to 7,500 words
A complete arc in a few scenes. Pressure builds, breaks, settles.
Long Short Story
7,500 to 20,000 words
Room for a subplot or a second viewpoint. Still tight.
Novella
20,000 to 40,000 words
A full arc with breathing room. Acts, turns, an interior.
