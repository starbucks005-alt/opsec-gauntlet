Summary of The Gauntlet and the Judges
The Gauntlet is a highenergy idea challenge where users submit their pitch and run it through a multistage evaluation powered by a cast of AI judges, each built as a character actor with a distinct personality, worldview, and decision style. It delivers the same excitement people associate with Shark Tank, but with deeper analysis, sharper insights, and a more immersive experience.
When a user enters The Gauntlet, their idea is tested across clarity, viability, risk, evidence, narrative strength, and audience impact. Each judge responds in their own voice, some tough, some warm, some skeptical, some visionary, creating a dynamic, multiangle critique that feels alive and unscripted.
These judges are not generic AI. They are AI character actors, designed with:
consistent personalities
stable evaluative lenses
unique communication styles
specialized expertise
predictable decision patterns
Together, they form a panel that challenges assumptions, highlights blind spots, and surfaces strengths the user may not have seen. The result is a fast, fun, personalitydriven evaluation that helps founders, creators, and innovators sharpen their ideas with clarity and confidence.
The Gauntlet is where ideas get tested by AI judges with real personalities, intense, insightful, and unforgettable.
Meet Your Judges
When you enter The Gauntlet, you don’t face a bland algorithm. You face a cast of AI characteractors built with real personalities, real opinions, and real evaluative styles. These judges don’t just score your idea. They react to it. They challenge it. They push it. They bring the same energy people expect from Shark Tank, but with sharper analysis and zero ego.
You choose three judges to run your idea through The Gauntlet. As you select them, they move from the shelf into their seats, taking their place on your personal panel. Each judge brings a different lens, a different temperament, and a different worldview.
Some are tough. Some are warm. Some are skeptical. Some are visionary. All of them are consistent, opinionated, and unforgettable.
Once seated, your judges become your evaluators for the entire run. They speak in their own voices, debate each other, and expose blind spots you didn’t know you had. They don’t always agree — and that’s the point. The tension between them is where the insight lives.
This is The Gauntlet. Your idea. Your judges. Your challenge.

13. Developer README
Purpose
This document defines the entire evaluation pipeline. All modules are JSON driven. No logic is hardcoded.
How to run the pipeline
Load orchestration_master.json
Follow pipeline_order
For each module:
Load config files
Pass required inputs
Store outputs
On failure, follow error_handling rules
Store all audit trail items
How to add a new module
Add module block
Add config files
Insert module id into pipeline_order
Ensure inputs are produced by earlier modules
Versioning
Increment orchestration version on any change
Store version in evaluations table
