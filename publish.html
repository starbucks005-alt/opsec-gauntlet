Publishing Pathway

Your Research.
Your Book.
Our Imprint.

Greylander Press accepts nonfiction submissions from authors who have completed substantive research in their field. We are not a vanity press. We publish work that earns the GP imprint.

Authors who complete the full NF Book Engine workflow through SLR Studio are invited to submit to Greylander Press at the conclusion of their manuscript development. The research you built there becomes the foundation we publish here.

We handle ISBN assignment, cover development, KDP print and ebook formatting, and ACX audiobook placement. You keep your rights. We provide the infrastructure and the imprint.
